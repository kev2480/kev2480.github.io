Festival


Download my dissertation!

The idea came up from this project when I noticed an annoying problem at the Boardmasters festival in Newquay 2014. It became apparent that after a storm had hit the festival the sunday had been cancelled. A problem quickly developed, there were large queues of people trying to enter the festival from rumours spreading that it might still be on.

Now if only there had been a quick and easy way for a small festival such as Boardmasters to send out a quick notification to everyone confirming the festival was over...

As part of my final year project I took on the challenge to develop a new way for small festivals to quickly publish and update mapping/line up and news straight to a festival goer's mobile device.
This was an eye openning experience for me as it helped me understand how the power of the web and smart phones can deliver useful information to users.

Technologies

  • Amazon Web Services
    (S3, RDS, EC2)
  • Developed with JAVA
    Desktop application & Web service
  • Android Development
    Mobile application
  • Google Cloud messaging
    To notify mobile users of new information
  • MySql
